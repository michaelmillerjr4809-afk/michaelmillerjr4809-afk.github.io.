Privacy Policy

This policy describes how CartoonMaker handles your data.

Overview

CartoonMaker runs entirely in your browser. Images, audio, and script text are processed locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store personal data on external servers.

Data Usage

  • Images: Used to render animations within the app. Not uploaded.
  • Script Text: Used for captions. Not uploaded.
  • Microphone (optional): If enabled, narration is captured locally for export. Not uploaded.
  • Background Music (optional): Mixed locally into exports. Not uploaded.

Cookies & Storage

The app may use local caching for performance and offline use (via Service Worker). No tracking or analytics cookies are used.

Third-Party Services

When enabled, the FFmpeg WebAssembly bundle runs locally in your browser to encode videos. No data is sent to third-party servers.

Contact

For questions, contact the developer of CartoonMaker.
